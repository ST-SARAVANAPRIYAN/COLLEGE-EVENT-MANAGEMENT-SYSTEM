Why set up UPI payments?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) allows your event participants to pay easily using apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, and others. Setting up UPI payments can increase your registration rate by making payments quick and convenient.

Enter your UPI ID in the format username@bankname or username@upi
Common UPI handles:
@okicici @okhdfcbank @ybl @upi @paytm @axl
